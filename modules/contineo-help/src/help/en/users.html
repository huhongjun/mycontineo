To add a Customer

The insertion mask contains all the descriptive data of a sure customer.
Some data obbligatori (are evidenced from the symbol asterisk (*)), in particular:

Every customer can belong to or more groups, the emergency criteria always are expressed in relation to the groups and never the single customers.
In order to select more groups to hold pressed key SHIFT and with the mouse to cliccare on the several groups.
Once compiled the fields to press the push-button customer Saves in order to confirm the new operation of creation.
