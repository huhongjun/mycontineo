Management Groups

A group is with of customers who share the same permissions; it is identified from an univocal name and it can have a description.

A type customer Administrator can approach the page of maintenance with the distance: Administration --> Groups.
In the main shielded one it will come shown the directory of the present groups on the system.
The possible operations are those usual ones of management: creation, modification, erasure.
